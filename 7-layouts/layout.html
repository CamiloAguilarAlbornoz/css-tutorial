Batman

He is a superhero from the DC comics publisher, created by Bob Kane in 1939, It has become one of the main references of popular culture, Next we will know its cinematographic history, without taking into account animated films

Batman 60s

Adam West was the first to play Batman in a fun series that started in 1966 and I conclude in 1968, it had quite a colorful tone, very different to which the character would have in later years

Batman, Batman Returns

Trying to get away from the colorful tone of the 60s series, Tim Burton made 2 films about the character during the years 1989 and 1992, being these "Batman" and "Batman Returns", which were quite successful.

Batman Forever

Warner, wanting to make the Batman movies more familiar, they replaced Tim Burton with Joel Schumacher, who gave the franchise a very similar tone to that of the 60s, beginning the decline of the saga.

Batman and Robin

Out of respect for the actors and actresses who were not to blame and only followed a horrible script, no image was placed in which these appear, spare To say that this is one of the worst movies ever created.

Batman, The Dark Knight, The Dark Knight Rises

After the failure of Batman and Robin, Chistopher Nolan set out to create a new trilogy about the character, resulting in one of the most successful, my favorite "The Dark Knight".

Batman v Superman, Suicide Squad , Justice League

In an attempt to copy Marvel's success with his cinematic universe, Warner tried to create his with DC choosing a new Batman, be it good or not on paper, bad decisions have not allowed this idea to take off.
